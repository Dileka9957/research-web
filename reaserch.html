Integrating Emerging Technologies for Comprehensive Parkinson's Disease Management

Innovative solutions to improve the quality of life for individuals with Parkinson's disease.

About Us

We are dedicated to leveraging cutting-edge technology to provide comprehensive management solutions for Parkinson's disease. Our multidisciplinary approach incorporates wearable devices, hypomimia, voice analysis, and mobile applications to offer real-time monitoring, early detection, and personalized care.

Research Gap

Despite significant advances in Parkinson's disease research, there remains a critical need for more effective, real-time monitoring solutions and early detection methods. Current approaches often lack the integration of advanced technologies to provide comprehensive and personalized care.

Research Problem

The primary challenge in managing Parkinson's disease lies in the timely and accurate detection of symptoms, as well as the continuous monitoring of disease progression. Existing methods are often invasive, costly, and limited in scope, leading to delayed interventions and suboptimal patient outcomes.

Our Services

Wearable Device for Real-Time Symptom Monitoring

Advanced sensor technologies and signal processing algorithms to provide real-time feedback and precise symptom assessment.

Facial Expression Detection

Enhanced hypomimia consultations with facial expression detection for better assessment of emotional and motor symptom fluctuations.

Voice and Speech Analysis for Early Detection

Machine learning algorithms to detect subtle changes in voice patterns and speech characteristics associated with early stages of Parkinson's disease.

Mobile App for Early Symptoms Detection, Monitoring, and Recommended Exercises

Continuous background analysis using gyro-sensors, notifications based on movement patterns, and exercise recommendations to minimize early stage effects.

Technologies Used

  • Wearable Devices: Incorporate sensors such as accelerometers and gyroscopes for precise motion tracking and real-time symptom monitoring.

  • Mobile Applications: Develop apps for continuous monitoring, exercise recommendations, and user engagement.
  • Data Analytics: Analyze large sets of patient data to identify patterns and improve predictive models.
  • Cloud Computing: Use cloud services for data storage, processing, and real-time access to information.
